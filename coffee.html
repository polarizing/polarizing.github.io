Coffee Ethan (2017)
Creative Coding / Animation
p5.js, Adobe Photoshop, pizzicato.js
Communications Lab
This project name is dedicated to a friend of mine, Ethan, who on the first day of our coding bootcamp when we had to introduce our first name along with a mnemonic that matched the first initial of our name, decided that C and E were the same letters and said, "I am Coffee Ethan".
In collaboration with Katie Lee
