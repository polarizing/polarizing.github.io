    DEVONagent
 

DEVONagent Pro helps you search more efficiently on the web. It searches multiple sources, frees you from hunting for the really relevant results, and gives you power tools for your research. DEVONagent Express and DEVONagent Lite (free) make searching the web as easy as using Spotlight. Search Google, Bing, YouTube, Facebook, Twitter, even the Mac App Store, right from the menu bar.
