Paper Crane Cards (2017)
Design Thinking, UI / UX
Sketch, Adobe Illustrator
People2, Shanghai, China
Target Corporation
Runner-up of the 2017 Target Hackathon -- Designing a Mobile Application for Left Behind Children in China. Awarded second-place prize of Â¥10000. The idea of the application is to facilitate and encourage communication between left behind children and their migrant parents through sending multimedia greeting cards -- these cards can have handwriting, photos, videos, and audio attached. An animated paper crane delivers these cards and encourages continued dialogue between the child and the parent.
In collaboration with Jackie Hu, Sam Li, Yuxin Wu.
