Odyssey (2016)
Networking, New Interfaces for Musical Expression
paper.js, HTML5 Canvas, socket.io
5 Hanover Square, New York, NY
Winner of the 2016 Fullstack Hackathon
Using paper.js and socket.io, four unique animations are rendered individually on phones and together on a host computer. The application creates different beats for different gestures from each phone and the animations play correspondingly. Sockets allow multiple phones to connect and jam out at the same time.
In collaboration with Ishaan Nagpal.
